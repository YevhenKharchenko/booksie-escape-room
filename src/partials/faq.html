FAQ

Before You Open the First Book…

  • How difficult is the game?

    The library is designed for intermediate-to-advanced puzzlers. Expect cryptography, riddles, and layered challenges.

  • Do I need to know Latin or ancient languages?

    No — all clues are solvable with logic. Translations are hidden within the game.

  • What if I get stuck?

    A game master will provide hints if needed.

  • Is it dark inside?

    The library is candlelit for atmosphere, but lanterns are provided for puzzles.

  • Can I book a private session?

    Yes, entire rooms can be reserved for private groups or team-building events.
