Story of the Library

A Place Where Knowledge Becomes a Challenge

The Booksie library is not just an escape room — it is a living story, carefully crafted to blur the line between fiction and reality. Imagine stepping into a forgotten hall where shelves stretch to the ceiling, each volume whispering secrets from centuries past.
Legends say that the library was once guarded by a secret order of scholars who believed that true wisdom must be earned through trials of the mind. They filled the hall with enchanted tomes, hidden locks, and puzzles that only the cleverest could solve. Many entered, but few left with the final key.

Now it’s your turn. You and your team are invited to:

  • Uncover mysteries hidden in forgotten manuscripts

  • Follow glowing runes that reveal secret paths

  • Unlock doors disguised as bookshelves

  • Race against time before the final bell chimes

Every page turned, every candle lit, and every riddle solved will draw you deeper into the story — until you either master the knowledge of the library or remain forever trapped in its secrets.
